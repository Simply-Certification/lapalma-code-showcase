Maria Sandoval

ITT Tech alumni • Founder of Developmental Diagnostics

CEO/Founder

Mrs. Sandoval was born in Los Angeles, CA November, 15, 1999. She became fascinated with computer science at age 11, when she got her first desktop. Upon graduation from Pasadena High School she applied for admission to ITT Tech. Once accepted she began her journey as a front end developer, reshaping the way developers collaborate. She assisted with the development of Facebook, Instagram, and Microsoft Teams. Those projects showed the world what an amazing software engineer she was, but no one knew all she had created. After she graduated ITT Tech she began developing cyber-security/anti-virus software in her spare time. She sold a few to various computer companies but one she just could not let go. She spent years perfecting it so to her it was to like a newborn to a mother. She founded Developmental Diagnostics and her software was the first product she offered. Today the company offers over 25 services, employs 300+ personnel and is on the softwares 5th installment. She revolutionized how people should protect their data and hardware. She is the Bill Gates of cyber-security. So please for your sake join her and be amazed at the ways you are left unprotected with the standard software you thought would suffice.
