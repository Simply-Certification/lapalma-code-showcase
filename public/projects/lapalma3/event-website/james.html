James Carter

MIT Magna Kum Laude • Lead developer at Apple Intelligence

AI programmer

James Carter was born in Austin, TX May, 07, 2003. He was different in many ways, while most kids enjoyed being outside playing he was disassembling and upgrading pc towers. He realized he wanted to learn more about them and the way they functioned his sophomore year. His junior year he applied for admission to MIT. During the summer months he was shocked with an acceptance letter. His first year at MIT he formed many friendships which proved to be beneficial. He partnered with people who went on to be developers at Apple, Microsoft, and LinkedIn. He wrote many programs over countless hours of collaboration with his buddies and learned his true calling, web development. He worked as a freelance engineer on the side while employed by Apple. He always owned an Iphone for as long as he can remember. Then one day he came up with the idea of integrating AI into his personal mobile device which was an Iphone 14 Pro. He showed off his hack at work and word got around, and before he realized what he had done he was in a conference room with half of the board members. They brainstormed and critiqued his installment on the device which they thought was revolutionary. Two months later the board approved funding for the project and elected him lead developer. He went on to create Apple Intelligence which debuted with IOS 18. Still today he runs the department when he isnt speaking at events like this raising awareness on AI and all it can offer. Join him for an event you wont want to miss because nobody knows artificial intelligence like James Carter.
