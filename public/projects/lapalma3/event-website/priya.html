Priya Patel

CEO at W3 Schools • Founder of The Future of Frameworks

Founder/CEO

Mrs. Patel originally from San Francisco, CA is a remarkable web developer. She is a graduate of The University of Arizona. She was also the lead developer at google before Google Chrome was launched. Her work there did so much for the company that it landed her an interview at W3 Schools. After 7 years at W3 Schools she decided she wanted to give kids who had less opportunity a chance at following her dreams. She founded The Future of Frameworks which is based out of San Diego, CA. The company's third anniversary marked the opening of a second and third location in Houston, TX and Atlanta, GA. Her work did not go unnoticed, she was not long after voted onto the board of directors at W3 Schools and rose to the chair of CEO within two years. She will be debuting her cyber-security/AI package which development began on while she was attending The University of Arizona. She will cover all the basics on cyber-security including protecting yourself from those who mine data.
